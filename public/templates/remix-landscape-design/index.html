Philosophy

Gardens that belong
to the land

I design landscapes that speak the dialect of their place — drought-tolerant plantings that honor California's rhythms, butterfly gardens that hum with life, and fire-resilient zones that protect what matters most.

Every line I draw begins with listening. To the soil, the light, the way water wants to move. The Napa Valley has its own vocabulary, and the best gardens are the ones that already feel like they've been here.
