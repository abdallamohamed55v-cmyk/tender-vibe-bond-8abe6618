SPIDER
ABILITIES

A radioactive spider bite granted Peter Parker extraordinary abilities beyond human limits.

🕸️

WEB-SLINGING

Custom web-shooters fire tensile webbing strong enough to restrain superhuman foes and swing between skyscrapers at incredible speeds.

⚡

A precognitive danger-detection ability that manifests as a tingling at the base of the skull, alerting to threats before they materialize.

Proportional strength of a spider — capable of lifting approximately 10 tons and delivering devastating strikes.
