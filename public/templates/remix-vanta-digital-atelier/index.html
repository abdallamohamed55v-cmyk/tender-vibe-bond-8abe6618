Who We Are

A studio built on
Restraint

VANTA is a generative design laboratory founded in 2020 at the intersection of computational art and cultural curiosity. We believe that the most powerful aesthetic experiences emerge not from decoration, but from deeply considered systems.

Our practice spans WebGL environments, physical installations, and brand identities — unified by a commitment to work that rewards attention. We don't chase trends. We build things that feel inevitable in retrospect.

Based between Lisbon and Berlin, we collaborate with cultural institutions, technology companies, and independent artists who share our appetite for the unexpected.

Our Beliefs

The Manifesto

I
Systems over Symbols

We distrust decoration. A beautiful system generates beauty without needing to be told what beautiful is. Logos that evolve by rule, not by hand — these are the artifacts we seek.

II
Emergence over Intent

The most surprising results come from stepping back and letting the logic breathe. We write the rules, then get out of the way. Curation happens after emergence.

III
Craft at the Pixel Level

Precision matters infinitely. A shader that's wrong by one line is entirely wrong. We care about the quality of silence between the notes as much as the notes themselves.

IV
Time as Material

Still images are statements. Time-based work is a conversation. We prefer conversations — things that change with the viewer, the hour, the data, the day.

V
Restraint as Radicalism

In a world of maximalism, doing less is the most disruptive act. We subtract until only the essential remains — and then we subtract once more.
