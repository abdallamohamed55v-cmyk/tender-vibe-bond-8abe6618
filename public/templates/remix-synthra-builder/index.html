Frequently Asked
Is it really free forever?+
Yes. The core product — unlimited websites, AI generation, global deployment — is and will always be free. We believe creation should be accessible to everyone.
Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. Describe what you want in plain language and Synthra builds it. If you do know how to code, you can also dive into the generated source at any time.
Can I connect my own domain?+
Absolutely. Point your DNS to our servers and your site is live on your domain within minutes. SSL is provisioned automatically at no extra cost.
How fast does the AI generate a website?+
Most websites are generated and live in under 30 seconds. Complex multi-page builds with custom animations may take up to 90 seconds.
Can I edit the site after it's generated?+
Yes — every element is editable. Use our visual editor, re-prompt the AI to refine sections, or export and edit the raw HTML/CSS/JS directly.
