What is Schema Markup?

The invisible code that
makes AI trust you

Schema markup (JSON-LD structured data) is a standardised vocabulary that tells AI systems and search engines exactly what your business offers — explicitly, not by inference.

📋
01
Machine-Readable Identity
AI systems stop guessing and start citing. Your name, address, services, and credentials are unambiguous.
🤖
02
Citation Authority
ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google SGE prefer sources with structured data — it's verifiable and trustworthy.
📈
03
Compound Visibility
Schema compounds over time — each new AI model trained on the web learns your entity and reinforces your authority.
