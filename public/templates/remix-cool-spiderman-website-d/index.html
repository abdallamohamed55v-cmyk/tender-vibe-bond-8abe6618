// Powers

SPIDER
ABILITIES

A radioactive spider bite granted Peter Parker extraordinary abilities beyond human limits.

WEB-SLINGING

Custom web-shooters fire tensile webbing strong enough to restrain superhuman foes and swing between skyscrapers at incredible speeds.

SPIDER-SENSE

A precognitive danger-detection ability that manifests as a tingling at the base of the skull, alerting to threats before they materialize.

SUPER STRENGTH

Proportional strength of a spider — capable of lifting approximately 10 tons and delivering devastating strikes.

WALL-CRAWLING

Electrostatic force between molecular boundary layers allows adhesion to virtually any surface at any angle.

// Multiverse

EVERY
SPIDER-MAN

ACROSS THE SPIDER-VERSE

The multiverse doesn't just contain alternate Earths — it contains alternate destinies. Every Spider-Man carries the same core truth: loss shapes the hero, and responsibility defines the mission.

From Peter B. Parker's weary wisdom to Gwen Stacy's graceful determination, each version of the spider hero reflects a different facet of courage under impossible odds.

The spider's thread connects them all — different worlds, same soul.
